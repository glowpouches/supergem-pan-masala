OUR STORY

From a small unit in Karnataka to every corner shop in the state.

Shivam Industries began with a simple belief — that Karnataka deserves a pan masala of its own: pure, saffron-blended, and completely free of tobacco and nicotine. Under the GM mark, we craft each batch with carefully selected supari, catechu, cardamom and real saffron.

Today, Supergem, Supergem Premium and Siddu are the trusted choice at thousands of shops across the state — a gem of taste in every pouch, at a price everyone can afford.

"A gem in taste, a mountain in trust"
FROM THE ARECA GROVES

Every pouch begins under these palms.

Our supari comes from the areca groves of Karnataka's malnad belt — tall palms tended by generations of farmers, harvested by hand and sun-cured before they reach our blending floor.
